Campus Ambassador Experience

As a Campus Ambassador, I have had the opportunity to represent leading platforms and connect students with exciting job opportunities, both in the tech space and entry-level job markets. Below are details of my contributions to these projects.

Campus Ambassador (Volunteer Work)

Kormo Jobs by Google | 05/2020 - 06/2022, Dhaka

As a Campus Ambassador for Kormo Jobs, I helped the Google-backed platform reach a wider audience at Southeast University by promoting the app and organizing events. Kormo Jobs connects job seekers with employers in emerging markets like Bangladesh, providing entry-level jobs to young professionals.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Promoted Kormo Jobs on campus and connected students with job opportunities in various fields.
  • Organized events to spread awareness about job opportunities and how to navigate the job market effectively.
  • Educated students on how to build strong resumes and profiles within the Kormo Jobs platform.

Impact:

Through my efforts, more than 200 students created accounts on Kormo Jobs, connecting them to job opportunities that matched their skill sets. The platform has now become a go-to resource for many of my peers who are seeking internships and entry-level jobs.

Campus Ambassador (Volunteer Work)

Shomvob Technologies Ltd. | 01/2023 - 04/2023

At Shomvob Technologies, I was part of the ambassador team responsible for promoting their Android application. The app was designed to help students and entry-level job seekers find relevant job openings in their areas of interest, primarily in the tech and service sectors.

Key Responsibilities:

  • Promoted Shomvob Android app on campus, increasing its visibility and encouraging students to download and use the app.
  • Demonstrated app features to students and explained how it could benefit them in their job search.

Impact:

Over the course of my tenure as an ambassador, I successfully onboarded more than 150 students onto the Shomvob platform, helping them discover new job opportunities and prepare themselves for interviews with local companies.
