Sales & Profit Dashboard

A professional dashboard for business insights, tracking key performance metrics, and visualizing sales and profit trends.

View Project on GitHub

Key Features

  • Key Metrics Overview: Highlight total sales and total profit for quick performance snapshots. This feature allows users to quickly assess the overall health of their business at a glance.
  • Profit by Year Chart: Visualize yearly profit trends to identify growth opportunities. This chart helps in understanding how profits have changed over the years, enabling strategic planning.
  • Sales Breakdown by Category: Dive deep into sales distribution across categories. This analysis helps businesses identify which categories are performing well and which may need attention.
  • Customer Count by Year: Analyze customer engagement trends with a detailed pie chart. Understanding customer growth or decline over the years can inform marketing and sales strategies.
  • Sales by State Map: Understand your geographical performance with a sales heatmap. This interactive feature allows users to visualize where their sales are coming from, helping to target specific regions for growth.
  • Monthly Sales Trends: Spot performance patterns with easy-to-read monthly trends. This feature aids in identifying seasonal trends and making informed inventory decisions.
  • Top 5 Customers Profits: Identify and focus on your most valuable customers. Recognizing high-value clients can lead to better customer relationship management and targeted marketing efforts.
