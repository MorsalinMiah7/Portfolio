Data Professional Survey Breakdown

A professional dashboard for business insights, tracking key performance metrics, and visualizing sales and profit trends.

View Project on GitHub

Key Features

  • Total Show Count: Comprehensive visualization showing a total of 9,685 Prime Video shows spanning from 1920 to 2021.
  • Ratings Distribution: Breakdown of shows based on different rating categories (13+, 16+, 18+, PG, etc.), helping analyze content suitability.
  • Genre Popularity: Insightful analysis showing Drama (2,216 shows) as the most dominant genre, followed by Action (1,657) and Comedy (1,475).
  • Country-Based Show Distribution: Interactive map displaying show availability and distribution across different countries.
  • Movies vs TV Shows: Categorization of content types, revealing that 80.82% of the content consists of TV shows while 19.18% are movies.
  • Release Year Trends: Historical trend analysis of show releases, highlighting peak production years.

Business Impact

This visualization provides valuable insights for content creators, streaming services, and entertainment analysts.
