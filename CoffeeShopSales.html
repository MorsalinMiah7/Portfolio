Coffee Shop Sales Analysis

A professional dashboard for business insights, tracking key performance metrics, and visualizing sales and profit trends.

View Project on GitHub

Key Features

  • Sales Performance Metrics: Comprehensive overview showing total sales ($698,812.33), total footfall (149,116), and average metrics per customer for quick business health assessment.
  • Hourly Sales Analysis: Detailed tracking of order quantities throughout the day, helping identify peak business hours and optimize staffing schedules.
  • Product Size Distribution: Visual breakdown of customer preferences for different sizes (Large 30%, Regular 31%, Small 30%), enabling better inventory management.
  • Category Performance Analysis: Detailed distribution showing Coffee (39%) as the leading category, followed by Tea (28%) and other products.
  • Top 5 Products Analysis: Tracking best-performing items like Barista Espresso ($91,406.20) and Brewed Black ($77,081.95) to optimize menu offerings.
  • Weekly Revenue Patterns: Analysis of daily revenue trends showing consistent performance across weekdays with peak earnings on Mondays ($101,677.28).
  • Store Location Performance: Comparative analysis across different locations (Astoria, Hell's Kitchen, Lower Manhattan) to identify successful store strategies.

Business Impact

This comprehensive sales analysis dashboard provides actionable insights for coffee shop operations. By visualizing key metrics and trends, it enables data-driven decisions for inventory management, staffing optimization, and menu planning. The analysis reveals valuable patterns in customer behavior and product performance, essential for strategic business growth.
