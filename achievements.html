Research Defence: Course Recommendation

Research Date: November 2024

The study tackles the cold start problem in course recommendations using a hybrid approach. It applies K-means clustering to identify popular courses and integrates graph-based, content-based, and popularity-based methods for accuracy. Using real student data from Southeast University, it ensures logical course selection while reducing data sparsity issues, proving its effectiveness in personalized recommendations.
